Before the battle, there was tragedy

After the lovely family dog ceased to be, the parents of the mournful child endeavored to bring little Buddy back to the mortal plane. Using ancient arcane sorcery on a thunder night, the couple sprang into action to break down the barriers between life and death. At the beginning, the taboo ritual went as planned, and it would have gone as well, if not for a intruding fly; only the small flapping of wings, and a sneeze as loud as thunder, were enough to break the containment of the spell. The canine remained in its box, but other tenants gave their first steps that night. In his great wisdom, the patriarch of the family decided to get every person at home to a cabin, until he finds how to revert the unintended craft, or the effect wears off - whatever happens first. With no authority at home, the new residents have zero restraints for experiencing their newly given vitality. Some of them form clans, some of them became loners. But one thing is for sure: when factions come to rise, conflict becomes inevitable. The once cozy household became the battleground for miniature guerrillas; and those pricks are pretty resourceful with their weapons. No Act GuyTM stayed with their arms crossed. No Fashion LassTM remained in one place. The forgotten ones wanted payback for their long slumber, and blamed the favorites for their ill fate. This is the dawn of a new type of conflict, to call it a war is misleading: this is a Toy Rampage.

Arm up and play!

Armed conflict has never seen a scale like this, and the participants are not capable of picking up a traditional weapon. These little warriors, using their meager resources and ingenuity, manage to battle. Tour the family home and destroy your enemies. Shoot first, shoot later.
